About

Built on thirty years of real decisions

Criterion Advisory Group is a Houston-based strategic advisory firm with a primary focus on nonprofits, physician-led foundations, and global health initiatives — organizations where the mission is the point, and where rigorous outside counsel can make a measurable difference.

The firm was built on a simple conviction: that the experience earned in the most demanding environments of commercial enterprise — capital structure, organizational crisis, high-stakes decision-making — is precisely what mission-driven organizations need and rarely have access to.

We don't work with everyone. We work with leaders who are serious about their mission and honest about the gap between where they are and where they need to be. That conversation requires trust, and trust requires that the advisor across the table has actually been somewhere. Ours has.

CAG is a boutique practice by design. Engagements are selective, attention is undivided, and Gary Quinn leads every engagement directly — drawing on a network of specialized professionals as the work requires.

Gary Quinn
Founder & Principal

Gary Quinn has spent more than three decades at the sharp end of American business — building companies from the ground up, steering through forces that would have broken most organizations, leading management consulting engagements across multiple industries, executing acquisitions, and securing financing on projects measured in the hundreds of millions.

That experience is not what drives him now. Having lived the full arc of commercial enterprise — its ambitions, its machinery, and its costs — he has made a deliberate choice to spend this chapter doing work that benefits people. Not as a retreat from complexity, but as a conscious application of hard-won judgment toward organizations whose success is measured in human outcomes rather than returns.

The clients he seeks are led by people of similar conviction: founders, physicians, and executives who are exceptional at their mission and want a seasoned advisor who can hold the strategic perimeter — someone who has seen enough to know what actually matters.

Work

Strategic advisory in practice

Flagship Engagement
Haiti
Bright Futures

501(c)(3) nonprofit dedicated to educational equity and youth development in Haiti. Co-founded by Dr. Steve Antoine, transplant cardiologist — DeBakey VA Houston and Baylor College of Medicine faculty.

CAG serves as Strategic Advisor to Haiti Bright Futures, a Candid Platinum-certified nonprofit navigating a complex operational and funding environment. The engagement encompasses organizational strategy, development planning, and a systems-level climate resilience initiative.

CAG produced Before the Water Rises — a comprehensive position paper documenting converging oceanographic and climate forces projected to precipitate a humanitarian crisis in Haiti within a 6–10 year horizon, and proposing a Seed Village architecture as a structured response. The document synthesizes peer-reviewed climate science with operational nonprofit strategy to make the funding case to institutional donors and mission-aligned investors.

Philosophy

How we work

Methodical

Every engagement begins with diagnosis, not prescription. We earn the right to recommend by understanding what is actually true before we say what should be done.

Precise

Vague strategy is not strategy — it is deferred accountability. We write recommendations that can be tested, measured, and owned by the people responsible for executing them.

Relentless

Complex problems require sustained attention. We stay in the work until the problem is solved, not until the deliverable is submitted. The relationship outlasts any single engagement.

Quiet

We have no interest in the spotlight. Our measure of success is the outcome our clients achieve — not the credit we receive for the work that made those outcomes possible.
